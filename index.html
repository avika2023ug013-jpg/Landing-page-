Designed for Touch

1. Touch to Identify

Unique tactile textures help identify the top, bottom, and latch immediately.

2. Open & Scoop

Assisted-open latches and curved scooping edges prevent spills.

3. Eat Independently

High contrast interiors make food visible, building true mealtime confidence.

Tactile Textures

Every surface tells a story. Distinct raised patterns guide little fingers to the right orientation instantly.

High Contrast Colors

Royal Blue exterior meets Saturated Yellow interior. This 7:1 contrast ratio helps low-vision children distinguish the box from the food.

The "Scoop" Wall

No more chasing food. One side of the container is curved upward, allowing spoons to catch food easily without spilling over.

Common Questions

Is it dishwasher safe?

Yes! Ehsaas is made from high-quality, BPA-free plastic that is top-rack dishwasher safe.

Does it work for total blindness?

Absolutely. The tactile markers on the lid and base allow children with zero vision to orient the box correctly by touch alone.
